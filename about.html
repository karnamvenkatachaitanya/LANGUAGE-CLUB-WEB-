About Us

The Language Club is one of the many clubs initiated by NBKRIST as a part of improving Language ability to express and discuss out-box thinking ideas among the students. It helps learners navigate their way through a world of linguistic diversity, to enable them to become autonomous and goal-focused learners who can acquire new language qualifications and develop the skills necessary to cope with multilingual situations and settings. This club is the platform to enhance their communication skills.

OBJECTIVES OF THE CLUB

  • To provide a more comprehensive and differentiated view of the linguistic reality of contemporary society.
  • To create an awareness of themselves as individuals with special different talents and capacities.
  • To give vent to students' creative talents.
  • Enhance students' learning opportunities the integration of language learning in personal development, and a more general validity of acquired knowledge and skills.
  • Engage them in cooperative tasks.
